# Upgradon Platform — Official Knowledge Base

Upgradon is an AI-powered career intelligence platform that bridges the gap between academics and industry.

## Features & Credit Costs
- **AI Career Roadmap**: Generates a step-by-step path to your target role.
  - Cost: 5 credits per generation.
- **Skill Gap Analysis**: Identifies missing skills and provides a learning plan.
  - Cost: 3 credits per analysis.
- **Resume Optimizer (Score)**: Scores your resume and provides ATS improvement tips.
  - Cost: 4 credits per score.
- **Resume Upload**: Upload PDF/DOCX for deep AI parsing and analysis.
  - Cost: 4 credits per upload.
- **AI Career Chatbot**: Real-time professional guidance and platform support.
  - Cost: 1 credit per message (Authenticated). Public chat is free but has shorter memory.

## Credit System
- **Signup Bonus**: New users receive 20 free credits upon registration.
- **Daily Refill**: Active users receive 10 free credits every day at midnight (UTC).
- **Referral Bonus**: Invite a friend using your unique link in the 'Referrals' tab. When they sign up, both of you earn +10 bonus credits.
- **Contribution Rewards**: Mark an applicant as 'Hired' on your Opportunity Board post to earn +5 reward credits.

## Opportunity Board
- You can post opportunities (Job/Internship/Project) for free.
- To connect with a poster, users must spend 1 connection credit (or use their free weekly limit).
- Credits are locked during the 'Pending' state and refunded if the request is 'Rejected' or 'Cancelled'.

## Contact & Support
For technical issues, contact support@upgradon.ai or use the Chatbot for immediate assistance.
